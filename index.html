About Us

Twisted Island is an upcoming animated webshow, starring a crew of anthropomorphic objects who compete challenges on a remote island for a currently unknown grand prize! The show is hosted by a slipper known as HelloKevin who is assisted by his badly mistreated crew, Cookie Straw, Dust Bunny, and Tape Recorder.
